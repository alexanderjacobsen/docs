Mantl is a drop-in chat sidebar that gives users an AI assistant inside your product. The assistant can call tools you define in your own code — read state, mutate state, navigate, fetch from your API — so it does real work rather than just answering questions.

## How it works

Mantl is split between a small SDK that runs in your app and a hosted backend that handles model calls, conversation persistence, and tool routing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="The SDK">
    `@mantl/react` provides a `MantlProvider` and a `MantlSidebar` component. Drop them into your app, register your tools, done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authentication">
    Your server signs short-lived JWTs using your project's secret key. The SDK fetches one when it mounts and uses it to authenticate against the hosted backend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The hosted backend">
    The backend talks to your chosen LLM provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.), persists conversations, and orchestrates tool calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your tools">
    When the AI decides to call a tool, the SDK invokes your local function in the browser, sends the result back, and the model continues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Mantl

- **Tools that actually do things.** The AI doesn't just answer — it can navigate, mutate state, hit your APIs.
- **Conversation history per user.** Built in. Users come back, their chats are still there.
- **Model-agnostic.** Configure OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or others per-project. Switch without code changes.
- **Themable to fit your product.** A handful of CSS variables let you blend the chat into your existing UI.
- **Type-safe.** Tools accept Zod or JSON Schema; inputs are validated before your code runs.
